# User Personas

## AI Usage

Use when designing UX, flows, or prioritizing features.

---

## Bride & Groom

Goals:

- Capture full wedding memory
- Control visibility and privacy
- Receive meaningful post-event artifacts

Pain Points:

- Missing candid guest photos
- Organizing large photo collections
- Ensuring safe sharing

---

## Guests

Goals:

- Upload content easily
- Share branded content
- Participate in wedding engagement

Pain Points:

- Complex onboarding
- Poor venue connectivity
- Lack of incentive to participate

---

## Wedding Planners

Goals:

- Manage ceremony phone usage
- Coordinate event timeline
- Enhance guest engagement
